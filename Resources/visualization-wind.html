Latitude vs Wind Speed


This chart is showing how latitude affects the wind speed. You can see that there is not a correlation between the wind speed and latitude.

Based on this chart we can make the conclusion that as we move further from the Equator, the wind speed is not directly affected. Wind is probably most influenced by topography (land features) of the area.
