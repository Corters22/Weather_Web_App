Latitude vs Temperature


This chart is showing how Latitude affects the temperature. You can see that there is in fact a correlation between maximum temperature and latitude. The Equator is located at 0 degrees and the temperature rises as it gets closer to the Equator.

Based on this chart we can make the conclusion that as we move further from the Equator, the temperature will continue to decrease. This explains why the North and South Poles have the lowest temperatures.
