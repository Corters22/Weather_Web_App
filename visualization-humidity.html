Latitude vs Humidity


This chart is showing how latitude affects the humidity of the area. You can see that there is not a correlation between the humidity and latitude.

Based on this chart we can make the conclusion that as we move further from the Equator, the humidity is not directly affected. Humidity is probably most influenced by whether or not the city is located near the coast.
